Classic Car Show 2024

September 10-12, 2024

Convention Center, Chicago

Event Details

Experience the nostalgia and beauty of classic automobiles at the Classic Car Show 2024. This three-day event showcases hundreds of vintage and classic cars from various eras.

Event Highlights

  • Over 500 classic cars on display
  • Rare and unique automobile exhibitions
  • Meet-and-greet with famous car collectors
  • Classic car auction
  • Restoration workshops

Daily Schedule

  • 9:00 AM - Doors Open
  • 11:00 AM - Guest Speaker Presentations
  • 2:00 PM - Classic Car Parade
  • 4:00 PM - Auction (Saturday Only)
  • 7:00 PM - Doors Close
